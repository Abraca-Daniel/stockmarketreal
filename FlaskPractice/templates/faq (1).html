Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Answers
Where can I see how much money I have available? You can see information about your account value in the Portfolio page.
Where can I buy/sell my stock? You can buy or sell stocks on the Trade page.
How do I add more money to my trading account? You can add more money in the Transaction page. There you can select an account to add money to.
Where can I see my latest purchase? You can view your recent order history in the Portfolio page under History.
Why can't I place an order? Most likely you either do not have enough money to buy the said quantity of that stock or the quantity requested is not available.
